## Usage

<DocSection>
  Dialogs are a good fit for confirmations, destructive actions, and short task-focused flows.

  <DocSubsection>
    <CompPreview
      code={`<Dialog>
  <DialogTrigger asChild>
    <Button>Open dialog</Button>
  </DialogTrigger>
  <DialogContent>
    <DialogHeader>
      <DialogTitle>Publish changes?</DialogTitle>
      <DialogDescription>
        Ship the updated registry and refresh the preview app.
      </DialogDescription>
    </DialogHeader>
    <DialogFooter>
      <DialogClose asChild>
        <Button variant="outline">Cancel</Button>
      </DialogClose>
      <DialogClose asChild>
        <Button>Publish</Button>
      </DialogClose>
    </DialogFooter>
  </DialogContent>
</Dialog>`}
    >
      <DialogInlinePreview />
    </CompPreview>
  </DocSubsection>
