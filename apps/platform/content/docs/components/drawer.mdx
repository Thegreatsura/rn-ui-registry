## Usage

<DocSection>
  Use a drawer when actions should rise from the bottom in a mobile-first way instead of interrupting with a centered modal.

  <DocSubsection>
    <CompPreview
      code={`<Drawer>
  <DrawerTrigger asChild>
    <Button variant="secondary">Manage project</Button>
  </DrawerTrigger>
  <DrawerContent>
    <DrawerHeader>
      <DrawerTitle>Project actions</DrawerTitle>
      <DrawerDescription>
        Choose the next step without leaving the current screen.
      </DrawerDescription>
    </DrawerHeader>
  </DrawerContent>
</Drawer>`}
    >
      <DrawerInlinePreview />
    </CompPreview>
  </DocSubsection>
</DocSection>
